# Similar Projects

This project isn't the only one of its kind. There are a couple of other projects that might also be of interest to you.

## Open Source Self-Hosted Projects

### [Wanderer](https://wanderer.to/)

Self-hosted activity tracker with ActivityPub support, allowing you to share activities via the Fediverse.

### [FitTrackee](https://docs.fittrackee.org/en/index.html)

Self-hosted activity tracker with file upload functionality and multi-user support, making it suitable for small groups or families.

### [CubeTrek](https://cubetrek.com/)

Self-hosted server for displaying activities with a focus on elevation data and 3D visualization of routes.

### [MyTourbook](https://mytourbook.sourceforge.io/mytourbook/)

Desktop application for visualizing and analyzing tours recorded with GPS devices or imported from fitness platforms.

### [Endurain](https://github.com/endurain-project/endurain)

Self-hosted fitness tracking service with a web interface and support for various activity file formats.

### [FitPub](https://feditrack.javahippie.net/timeline)

Bridges fitness tracking with the Fediverse, publishing activities to ActivityPub-compatible platforms.

## Recording

### [OpenTracks App](https://opentracksapp.com/)

Records activities on Android with GPS tracking, available via the F-Droid store. Exports to standard formats compatible with this project.

## Tile Hunting

### [Explorer Helper](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ru.anisart.vv)

Android app for tile hunting that overlays explored tiles on a map, helping you plan routes to new areas.

### [Wandrio](https://wandrio.net/)

Android and iOS app for tile hunting with a community leaderboard and route planning features.

## Local Analysis (No Cloud)

### [Running Free by Michael Simons](https://biking.michael-simons.eu/)

A personal project for tracking and visualizing cycling and running activities without sending data to the cloud.

### [Strava local heatmap](https://github.com/remisalmon/Strava-local-heatmap)

Python script that generates a local heatmap from Strava activity exports, keeping your data on your own machine.

## Analysis with Cloud Data

### [Statshunters](https://www.statshunters.com/)

Statistical analysis of activities synced from Strava, including tile hunting features and detailed segment statistics.

### [VeloViewer](https://veloviewer.com/)

In-depth statistical analysis of Strava activities with a focus on cycling, featuring segment explorer and achievement tracking.

### [Wandrer](https://wandrer.earth/)

Tracks which streets and roads you have covered and challenges you to explore every street in your city or region.

## Cloud Platforms

### [Strava](https://www.strava.com/)

The most popular social fitness platform for cyclists and runners, with segment leaderboards and a large community.

### [Komoot](https://www.komoot.com/)

Route planning and navigation platform with a focus on outdoor activities, offering curated tours and community highlights.
